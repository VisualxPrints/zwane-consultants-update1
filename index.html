Mobirise
Maduduza Zwane Labour Law Consultants and Associates (MZLLCA) was founded in 2006 and our purpose was to create a small outfit to provide business advisory services in Swaziland.
Within this short period we have become one of the largest independent firms of industrial relations practitioners and labour law consultants.
The rapid growth has been achieved through the quality of work and more importantly through dedicated qualified men and women committed to a tradition of dynamic professionalism.
For over three and half years we have offered original, innovative and resourceful approaches to the complex labour law and Industrial Relations challenges faced by our clients.
We have developed a team of highly specialized, skilled and committed consultants and offer a diverse range of labour law and industrial relations services.
